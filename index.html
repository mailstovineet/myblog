Must visit places all around the world

Disclaimer: I have not been to some of these places



Big Island, Hawaii

The Big Island in Hawaii is a scenic location with pristine beaches and turquoise waters. Other attractions there include Volcano National Park, which has lava tubes and volcano eruption sites, and also the luxurious tropical resorts.

Alaska

Alaska is a great place to go if you've never experienced snow before. There is lots of untouched wilderness and wildlife. You may see bears in their natural habitat. It is a camper's paradise.

Books + TV Review

Phineas and Ferb

"Phineas and Ferb" is about two brothers and their friends who build complex, crazy machines every day in summer vacation. The boys' sister, Candace tries to tell their mom that they're doing something dangerous, but by the time she looks, the contraption has always disappeared. Their pet platypus, Perry is a secret agent who fights evil and nobody knows about it.








harry potter

Harry Potter is a boy that is sent to a school for wizards. His abusive aunt and uncle, who are normal people, try to keep him away from it. He discovers that his parents died because of an evil wizard named Voldemort and that he is the only one who has survived Voldemort's spell. He tries to stop him in this seven-book series.
